Glacier Fish Company

Seattle, WA

Bruce Smith and his company, Glacier Fish Company, had been marketing hook-and-line caught wild salmon from southeast Alaska since 1978. Triad fish are caught using slow hook-and-line method to minimize stress, processed immediately on-board, and frozen within 90 minutes. Each fish is then individually hand dipped in fresh seawater to create a protective glaze and tagged to ensure each fish is traceable to the source.

This map depicts where each of our partner fishing boats are currently located. We use this to ensure the fishermen are meeting our sustainability requirements and to show where their fish originated from.
