Fresh, sustainably caught by local fisherman.

Our mission is to connect consumers with seafood harvested by local and independent fishermen to build the marketplace for seafood that is sustainable by rewarding fishermen with higher margins for thinking about future generations.

At ReelFish we make the seafood industry transparent, so the customer is as informed as possible about their purchase.

For each fish, we help the customer to understand:

Which boat they bought it from
Where their fish was caught
Health of the fish stock
Environmental impact of this purchase
